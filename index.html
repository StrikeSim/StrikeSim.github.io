THE NEED TO PROTECT WORKERS' RIGHTS

The globalization of businesses and effects of the burgeoning “sharing” or “gig” economy have challenged traditional labor relations between employee and employer. Unions, more than ever, are vital to reducing wage inequality, providing a platform for change, improving the standard of living for the working class, and ensuring safe working conditions.
