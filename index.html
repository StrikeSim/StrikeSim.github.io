THE SHARING ECONOMY

The growth of the sharing or "gig" economy allows us to rent goods and services from our peers with just the click of the button.

THE NEED TO PROTECT WORKERS' RIGHTS

However, headlines such as Huffington Post's How the Sharing Economy Screws American Workers and the New York Times' In the Sharing Economy Workers Find Both Freedom and Uncertainty reflect a growing concern that workers (at the price and convenience of their customers) risk exploitation due to the inability to unionize.

RISE OF VIRTUAL UNIONS

Consequently, it stemmed the birth of virtual unions. Organized online, virtual unions not only serve as a platform to promote fair wages, safe workplaces and decent work hours but when the need arises, plan a strike to draw national attention to worker rights.
